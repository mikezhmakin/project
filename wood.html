Types Of Wood

This will be an informative page about the diffrent types of wood.

Pine

Pine is the general wood planks you can find in your local hardware store. While good for most projects, it is soft.

Oak

Oak is a fairly rare but very good wood. It is hard, good for any project, and beautiful.

Hickory

Hickory is very stong and mostly used in tool and axe handles.

Poplar

Poplar is probobally the best wood for projects, it is light, easy to work with, and very smooth.

Why Does The Type Of Wood Matter?

Well the type of wood you pick for your project is very important. Say you want to make the second project (a mallet) and you use a soft wood for the handle. It would break in no time.
